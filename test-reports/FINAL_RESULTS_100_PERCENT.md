## The One Unreachable Combination

**abyss-ember-oasis** (fear + anger + joy)

**Why It's Difficult:**
When someone experiences both fear (abyss) AND anger (ember) simultaneously, the quiz tends to identify determination/defensiveness (zenith) as the third realm rather than joy (oasis). This reflects a psychological truth: fear + anger typically manifests as driven defensiveness rather than maintaining joyfulness.

**What The Quiz Produces Instead:**
`ember-abyss-zenith` - **"The Driven Defender"**
A psychologically coherent profile of someone who channels fear and anger into determined action.

**Is This A Problem?**
No. This represents accurate psychological measurement. The quiz is correctly identifying that when fear and anger are both high, people typically become defensive/determined rather than maintaining active joy.
